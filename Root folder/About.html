About Us

Zanele Zaynab Nkutha , the founder of ROOTD in UBUNTU, suffered from a chronic migraine for years. She did seek medical help, but the conventional medicine was not effective, and it was expensive. She decided to seek help through the ancient way which is using traditional remedies that our ancestors used to use and it was difficult to excess the medication since there are no ecommerce websites that sells traditional medicine , that is when she had the idea of creating the ROODT in UBUNTU organization.

In 2025, the ROOTD in UBUNTU was established for those who seek help through traditional remedies as an alternative option when conventional medicines are not effective. Our story is just beginning. We are building a future where South African traditional medicine is the global benchmark for holistic health.
