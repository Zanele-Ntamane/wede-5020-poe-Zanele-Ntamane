## Project Overview

Founding narrative of the organization

Zanele Zaynab Nkutha , the founder of ROOTD in UBUNTU, suffered from a chronic migraine for
years. She did seek medical help, but the conventional medicine was not effective, and it was
expensive. She decided to seek help through the ancient way which is using traditional remedies
that our ancestors used to use and it was difficult to excess the medication since there are no
ecommerce websites that sells traditional medicine , that is when she had the idea of creating the
ROODT in UBUNTU organization. <br>

In 2025, the ROOTD in UBUNTU was established for those who seek help through traditional
remedies as an alternative option when conventional medicines are not effective. <br>

Our story is just beginning. We are building a future where South African traditional medicine is the
global benchmark for holistic health.<br>

Mission:
 To provide a standardized, high-quality traditional remedies designed for a modern
lifestyle. 

Vision:
 To bridge the gap between African heritage and global health by becoming a well-known
source of African botanical wellness.

Target audience

• People seeking alternative options when conventional medicine does not work <br>
• People who prefer using natural herbs then western medications <br>


## Website Goals and Objectives

Goals for the website

• To establish brand authority and trust: To provide verified information about indigenous
plants and their uses.<br>
• Direct-to-consumer sales: To create a seamless journey that turns visitors into customers <br>
• To generate online sales of herbal medicine products through a fully functional ecommerce platform <br>

Objectives:
• To drive sustainable business growth by facilitating online sales and expanding customer 
reach <br>
• Simple product discovery, clear categories, good images and descriptions <br>
• Provide a 24/7 accessibility, customers can browser and buy anytime <br>
• To sell outside our local area <br>

Key performance indicators

• Customer Retention Rate- 60% of customers returning to purchase the products will
validate the efficiency of our remedies <br>
• Conversion Rate- 2-4% of visitors to make a purchase <br>
• Customer service response time- to to reply less than 2 hours in business hours <br>
